Pursuant to Section 12, Subsection 3 of the Administrative Procedures Act, in conjunction with the corresponding state regulation, the applicant is obligated to submit all documentation required for the processing of the application in certified form. Processing cannot commence until all documents have been submitted in their entirety.

The deadline for submission of documentation is six weeks from receipt of this notice. Failure to comply with the deadline may result in the application being considered void, unless an extension has been requested and approved.
