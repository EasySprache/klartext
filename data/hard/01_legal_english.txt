The Department of Justice and the Office of Legal Counsel provide for interested citizens access to nearly the entire body of current federal law at no cost via the Internet. The statutes and regulations may be retrieved in their currently applicable form. They are continuously consolidated by the documentation unit within the Office of Legal Counsel.

The use of the database is free of charge. Commercial use of the documents requires prior consent from the Department of Justice. Inquiries should be directed to the Department of Justice, Office of Public Affairs.
